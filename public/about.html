Our History

Stemcell México was born from a deep conviction: That regenerative medicine represents the future of human health. Inspired by scientific advances and the impressive clinical benefits of cell therapy, a group of healthcare professionals, researchers, and entrepreneurs decided to found this company in Tijuana, Mexico.

We chose Tijuana as our base for being a strategic city due to its medical growth and international connection, located in the prestigious DEL PRADO MEDICAL TOWER medical center.
