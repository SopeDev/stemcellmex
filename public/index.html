About Us

Our Story

Stemcell México was born from a deep conviction: that regenerative medicine represents the future of human health. Inspired by scientific advances and impressive clinical benefits of cell therapy, a group of healthcare professionals, researchers and entrepreneurs decided to found this company in Tijuana, Mexico.

Mission

To offer high-quality regenerative treatments based on stem cell therapies, with an ethical, scientific and personalized approach, to improve the quality of life of our patients.

Vision

To be leaders in regenerative medicine in Latin America, recognized for medical excellence, scientific innovation and human commitment.
