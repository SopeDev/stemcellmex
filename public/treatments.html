Mesenchymal Stem Cells

The foundation of modern regenerative medicine

Mesenchymal stem cells are specialized cells that have the unique ability to differentiate into different types of tissues and promote natural body regeneration. They are like the 'architects' of our body, capable of repairing and regenerating damaged tissues.

The treatment begins with a complete medical evaluation. Stem cells are obtained from certified sources and processed in top-level laboratories. The application is performed in a minimally invasive manner, specifically directed to areas requiring regeneration.

Key Benefits

  • Regeneration of damaged tissues
  • Reduction of chronic inflammation
  • Enhanced immune system
  • Accelerated healing process

Treatment Information

Duration: 1-2 hours
Sessions: 1-3 as needed
Recovery: Immediate
Results: 4-6 months

Exosome Therapy

Optimized cellular communication for advanced regeneration

Exosomes are microscopic extracellular vesicles containing proteins, lipids, and nucleic acids. They act as cellular messengers, facilitating communication between cells and promoting regeneration and rejuvenation processes at the molecular level.

This advanced therapy offers multiple advantages: it's minimally invasive, doesn't require general anesthesia, has a very short recovery time, and results are faster and longer-lasting than other regenerative treatments.

Key Benefits

  • Cellular rejuvenation
  • Enhanced immune function
  • Reduction of aging signs
  • Accelerated recovery

Treatment Information

Duration: 30-60 minutes
Sessions: 1-2 recommended
Recovery: Immediate
Results: 2-4 weeks

Intravenous Therapy

Direct nutrition to bloodstream for immediate results

Intravenous therapy is a method of administering nutrients, vitamins, and minerals directly into the bloodstream. This ensures 100% absorption, avoiding the loss that occurs in oral digestion and providing immediate and effective results.

Each IV treatment begins with a personalized evaluation of your nutritional needs. Our specialists design a specific vitamin cocktail that is administered safely and comfortably in a relaxing environment.

Key Benefits

  • Direct absorption into bloodstream
  • Immediate results
  • Enhanced energy and vitality
  • Immune system boost

Treatment Information

Duration: 40-60 minutes
Sessions: 1-6 as needed
Recovery: Immediate
Results: Immediate
